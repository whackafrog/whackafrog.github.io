WhackAFrog

Each time the frog plays, a number will be played shortly after. You then have to press that number, either on-screen, with the numbers across your keyboard from 1-9 or using the number pad to the right side of your keyboard however, fly noises will randomly play and a fly will fly across your screen. You get 2 points for hitting the frog and 1 for missing or leaving it too long. You get anywhere from 2 to 4 seconds, depending on the mode you choose and in the extra hard and extremely hard modes you have to whack the last number to play.
